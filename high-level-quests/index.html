Flowing Black Silk Sash (FBSS)

Level 41–50

Zones: Plane of Fear

The most coveted haste item in classic EverQuest, the FBSS drops in the Plane of Fear and provides a 36% haste effect. Nearly every melee and hybrid character pursues this item.

Zones: Nagafen's Lair / Permafrost Caverns
